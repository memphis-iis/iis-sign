IISSO Eye-Tracking Workshop

October 8th FIT 405 1-5 pm

The IIS student organization is hosting an Eye-Tracking Workshop on October 8th in FIT 405 from 1-5pm.

The workshop will feature student research presentations and hands-on equipment demonstrations with a Tobii Pro representative.

All experience levels with eye-tracking are encouraged to attend. Email Kelsey Mankel if you have any questions: kmankel@memphis.edu. RSVP: https://go.tobiipro.com/umemphisworkshop
